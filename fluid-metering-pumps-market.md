<p><strong>The Fluid Metering Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diaphragm Metering Pump</li><li>Plunger Metering Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fluid metering pumps market is primarily categorized into diaphragm metering pumps and plunger metering pumps. Diaphragm metering pumps use a flexible diaphragm to create a suction effect, ensuring precise flow control and minimal backflow, making them ideal for corrosive fluids. In contrast, plunger metering pumps utilize a plunger mechanism to move fluid, providing high pressures and suitable for viscous liquids. Both types cater to various industries, including chemicals, pharmaceuticals, and water treatment, emphasizing accuracy and reliability in fluid handling.</p></p>
