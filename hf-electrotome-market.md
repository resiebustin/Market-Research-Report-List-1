<p><p>The HF Electrotome market is primarily segmented into two types: unipolar and bipolar. </p><p>Unipolar electrotomes utilize a single active electrode to deliver high-frequency current, typically used for cutting or coagulating tissue with minimal lateral thermal damage. In contrast, bipolar electrotomes feature two electrodes, allowing current to flow between them, which provides precise control and reduces the risk of unintended tissue injury. Both types cater to different surgical needs, enhancing efficiency and safety in various medical procedures.</p></p>
