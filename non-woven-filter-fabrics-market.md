<p><ul><li>Needle Punched Nonwoven</li><li>Spunbond Nonwoven</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The non-woven filter fabrics market is primarily segmented into two types: needle-punched nonwoven and spunbond nonwoven. Needle-punched nonwoven fabrics are created by mechanically entangling fibers, resulting in a strong, durable material suitable for high-performance filtration applications. Spunbond nonwoven fabrics, on the other hand, involve spinning polymer fibers and bonding them through thermal or chemical processes, offering a lighter, versatile option. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, construction, and healthcare, driven by increasing filtration standards and environmental concerns.</p></p>
