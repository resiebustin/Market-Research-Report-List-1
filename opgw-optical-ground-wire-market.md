<p><strong>The OPGW (Optical Ground Wire) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Central Tube Structure OPGW</li><li>Layer Stranding Structure OPGW</li></ul></p>
<p><p>OPGW (Optical Ground Wire) serves a dual purpose of providing grounding and communication capabilities in overhead power lines. The Central Tube Structure OPGW features optical fibers housed centrally within a protective aluminum or steel layer, offering high mechanical strength and minimal signal attenuation. In contrast, the Layer Stranding Structure OPGW consists of several optical fibers layered around a central steel core, enhancing flexibility and reducing weight. Both types cater to distinct market needs depending on installation environments and performance requirements.</p></p>
