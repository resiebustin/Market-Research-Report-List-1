<p><strong>The Spray Dryer Absorber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotary Atomizer</li><li>Two-Fluid Nozzle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Spray Dryer Absorber market encompasses two main types: Rotary Atomizers and Two-Fluid Nozzles. Rotary Atomizers employ a spinning mechanism to generate fine droplets, effectively enhancing drying efficiency and ensuring uniform particle size. In contrast, Two-Fluid Nozzles utilize a combination of air and liquid to produce droplets through pressurized flow, offering flexibility and lower operational costs. Both technologies are critical in various industries, including pharmaceuticals, food processing, and chemical production, for effective drying and absorption processes.</p></p>
