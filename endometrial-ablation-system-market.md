<p><p>The endometrial ablation system market comprises several types targeting abnormal uterine bleeding. Electrical or electrocautery uses heat to destroy uterine lining, while hydrothermal employs heated fluid for similar effects. Balloon therapy utilizes a balloon filled with fluid to ablate tissue, and radiofrequency ablation employs radio waves for precise heating. Cryoablation relies on extreme cold to destroy cells, and microwave ablation uses microwave energy for targeted tissue removal. Each method offers unique benefits and is chosen based on patient needs and clinical conditions.</p></p>
