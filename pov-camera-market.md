<p><p>The POV camera market primarily features two types: CMOS and CCD cameras. CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) cameras are known for their low power consumption, fast processing speeds, and compact designs, making them suitable for action sports and mobile applications. In contrast, CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) cameras offer superior image quality and low noise, making them ideal for professional filmmaking and scientific imaging. Each type caters to different segments, influencing their deployment in various environments and applications.</p></p>
