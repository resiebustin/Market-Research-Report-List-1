<p><ul><li>Single-stage Torque Converter</li><li>Multistage Torque Converter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic transmission torque converter market features two main types: single-stage and multistage torque converters. Single-stage torque converters utilize a simple design with a single set of components, providing sufficient torque multiplication for standard applications. In contrast, multistage torque converters incorporate multiple components that allow for better efficiency and performance across varying speeds and loads. This versatility makes multistage designs suitable for high-performance vehicles, enhancing acceleration and overall drivability, while single-stage converters are often favored for cost-effectiveness and simplicity.</p></p>
