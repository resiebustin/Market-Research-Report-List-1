<p><p>Peristaltic pumps are essential in chemical processing, employing a flexible tube and a mechanism to move fluids. Two main types dominate the market: roller peristaltic pumps, which use rollers to compress the tubing, ensuring gentle fluid handling and precise flow rates; and shoe-type peristaltic pumps, which utilize a shoe to create a smooth compression, allowing for higher flow rates and reduced pulsation. Both types are designed for reliability and efficiency, making them ideal for various chemical applications.</p></p>
