Recycling transforms plastic waste into reusable materials, promoting circular economy practices and reducing resource depletion. Incineration involves burning plastic waste to generate energy, but it can release harmful emissions. Each method has distinct impacts on waste reduction, resource conservation, and environmental sustainability.</p></p>
