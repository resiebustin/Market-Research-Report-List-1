<p><p>The disposable dental cartridge syringe market is categorized into three main types: aspirating, non-aspirating, and self-aspirating syringes. Aspirating syringes allow dental professionals to confirm the injection site by creating negative pressure, ensuring safety. Non-aspirating syringes do not have this feature and are typically used for straightforward applications. Self-aspirating syringes feature an integrated mechanism that automatically creates suction when injected, simplifying the process and enhancing user efficiency. Each type is designed to enhance precision and safety in dental procedures.</p></p>
