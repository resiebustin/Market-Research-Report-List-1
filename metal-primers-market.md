Iron oxide primers are known for their excellent corrosion resistance and durability, making them ideal for steel surfaces and outdoor applications. In contrast, zinc chromate primers offer superior adhesion and inhibit rust formation, widely used in aerospace and automotive sectors. Both primer types serve as essential protective coatings, enhancing the longevity and performance of metal substrates exposed to harsh environments.</p></p>
