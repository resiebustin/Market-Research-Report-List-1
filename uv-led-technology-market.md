<p><p>The UV LED technology market is segmented into UVA, UVB, and UVC types based on their wavelengths. UVA LEDs emit long wavelengths (320-400 nm) and are commonly used in curing applications and for tanning. UVB LEDs (280-320 nm) are employed in therapeutic applications and sterilization, particularly for their germicidal properties. UVC LEDs (100-280 nm) are highly effective for disinfection, targeting viruses and bacteria in air, water, and surfaces. Each segment caters to specific industrial and healthcare needs.</p></p>
