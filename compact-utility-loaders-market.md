<p><p>Compact utility loaders are categorized primarily into two types: track and wheel loaders. Track loaders feature continuous tracks that offer enhanced stability and traction, making them suitable for uneven terrains and wet conditions. They excel in demanding environments like construction sites. In contrast, wheel loaders have a set of wheels that provide greater speed and maneuverability on smooth surfaces, making them ideal for urban settings and landscaping tasks. Each type serves specific applications, catering to diverse user needs and operational environments.</p></p>
