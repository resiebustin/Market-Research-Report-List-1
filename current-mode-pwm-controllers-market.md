<p><p>Current Mode PWM controllers are key components in power conversion, enabling efficient voltage regulation. The AC-DC Current Mode PWM Controllers facilitate converting alternating current into direct current, commonly used in power supplies for electronic devices. In contrast, DC-DC Current Mode PWM Controllers manage the conversion between different levels of direct current voltage, essential for battery-backed systems and power management in various electronic applications. Both types optimize operational stability, response speed, and reduce electromagnetic interference in power supply circuits.</p></p>
