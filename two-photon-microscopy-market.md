<p><p>The two-photon microscopy market is divided into in vivo and in vitro categories. In vitro microscopy, on the other hand, involves imaging samples outside their biological context, such as cell cultures or tissue slices, providing controlled environments for detailed analysis of cellular mechanisms and interactions.
