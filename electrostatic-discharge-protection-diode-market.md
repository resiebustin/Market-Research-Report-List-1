<p><p>The Electrostatic Discharge Protection Diode market comprises two main types: unidirectional and bidirectional ESD protection diodes. Unidirectional ESD protection diodes are designed to protect sensitive components from voltage surges in one direction, making them suitable for applications where the current flow is predominantly in one direction. In contrast, bidirectional ESD protection diodes can handle voltage surges from both directions, offering comprehensive protection for circuits susceptible to oscillating voltages, thus enhancing reliability in various electronic applications.</p></p>
