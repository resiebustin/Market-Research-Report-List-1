<p><p>The psychiatric care beds market comprises three main types: electric beds, semi-electric beds, and manual beds. Electric beds are fully adjustable through a motorized system, allowing precise positioning for patient comfort and care. Semi-electric beds combine manual adjustments for the upper body with electric controls for the lower sections, offering a balance of flexibility and ease of use. Manual beds require physical effort for adjustments, providing a cost-effective option while allowing caregivers to maintain control during patient care and treatment.</p></p>
