<p><p>The macroporous ion exchange resin market comprises two primary types: anion exchange resin and cation exchange resin. Anion exchange resins are designed to capture and remove negatively charged ions from solutions, making them essential for water treatment, metal recovery, and wastewater management. Cation exchange resins, on the other hand, target positively charged ions, commonly used in softening water, deionization, and separation processes. Both types are critical in various industrial applications, enhancing efficiency in chemical processes and environmental sustainability.</p></p>
