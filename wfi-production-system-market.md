<p><p>The WFI (Water for Injection) production system includes various market types such as Vapor Compression Distillation, Reverse Osmosis, and Ultrafiltration (UF). Vapor Compression Distillation involves evaporating and condensing water to achieve high purity. Reverse Osmosis employs semi-permeable membranes to remove contaminants, while Ultrafiltration further enhances water purity by removing larger particles and macromolecules. These technologies are critical in the pharmaceutical and biotechnology industries, ensuring the production of sterile, high-quality water necessary for medications and injectable formulations.</p></p>
