<p><strong>The Live Vaccines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monovalent Live Vaccines</li><li>Multivalent Live Vaccines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The live vaccines market is categorized into monovalent and multivalent live vaccines. Monovalent live vaccines target a single pathogen, providing focused immunity against specific diseases, making them effective for individual vaccinations. In contrast, multivalent live vaccines combine multiple attenuated pathogens into one formulation, offering broader immunity and convenience by reducing the number of vaccinations needed. This distinction allows for tailored vaccination strategies to enhance public health by addressing both specific and multiple infectious disease threats.</p></p>
