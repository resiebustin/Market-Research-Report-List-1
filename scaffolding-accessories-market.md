<p><p>The scaffolding accessories market includes various types of structures designed for temporary support during construction activities. Single scaffolding is a simple system often used for wall work; double scaffolding provides additional stability for taller buildings. Cantilever scaffolding extends from a structure, ideal for narrow spaces. Suspended scaffolding hangs from a roof or overhead support, suitable for high-rise works. Trestle scaffolding features movable legs for easy adjustment, while steel scaffolding is known for its durability and load-bearing capacity, making it popular across projects.</p></p>
