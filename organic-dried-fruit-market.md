<p><p>The organic dried fruit market is categorized into two main types: naturally dried and artificially dried. Naturally dried fruits are processed without the use of preservatives or chemicals, relying on methods like sun-drying or air-drying to retain their nutritional quality and flavor. In contrast, artificially dried fruits often involve the use of dehydrators or freeze-drying techniques, sometimes incorporating additives for enhanced color or shelf life. Both types cater to health-conscious consumers, with a growing preference for organic options free from artificial ingredients.</p></p>
