<p><strong>The Organic Canola Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressed</li><li>Leaching</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The organic canola oil market consists of two primary extraction methods: pressed and leached. Pressed canola oil is obtained through mechanical pressing, where seeds are crushed to release oil, preserving natural flavors and nutrients. In contrast, leaching involves using solvents to extract oil from seeds, which is then refined for purity. While pressed oil is valued for its minimal processing, leached oil typically has a higher yield but may undergo further refining, impacting its nutritional profile.</p></p>
