<p><p>The Roller Hearth Kiln market primarily comprises two types: Continuous Kilns and Batch Kilns. Continuous Kilns operate with a constant flow of material, allowing for uninterrupted processing and high efficiency, making them suitable for large-scale production. In contrast, Batch Kilns process materials in discrete batches, offering flexibility in production but often at lower throughput levels. Each type serves specific industrial applications, influenced by the required production volume, energy efficiency, and material handling needs. Both play crucial roles in various manufacturing sectors.</p></p>
