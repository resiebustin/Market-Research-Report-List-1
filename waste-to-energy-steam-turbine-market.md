<p><p>The Waste-to-Energy Steam Turbine market comprises various types, including condensing, back pressure, and others. Condensing turbines maximize energy extraction by allowing steam to expand fully and condense, optimizing efficiency. Back pressure turbines, on the other hand, utilize exhaust steam to provide heating or process energy, catering to specific industrial requirements. Other types may include combined cycle systems or regenerative thermal systems, which integrate additional technologies for enhanced performance. Each type serves distinct operational needs within waste-to-energy applications.</p></p>
