Electrodes and filler materials provide the necessary material to join metals, while fluxes help prevent oxidation during welding. Wires are used in processes like MIG welding for continuous feeding of filler material. Gases, such as argon and oxygen, are crucial for shielding the weld area and enhancing the welding process. Together, these materials ensure high-quality and efficient welding operations across industries.</p></p>
